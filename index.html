Research Interests

My research interest is to develop correct-by-construction AI algorithms with advanced reasoning capabilities. My focus areas are:

  • Reactive Synthesis

  • Game and Hypergame Theory

  • Formal Logic: Temporal, Epistemic and Spatial Logic

  • Applications: Cybersecurity, Robot Motion Planning

About me

I am a third year Ph.D. student in Robotics Engineering Program at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), advised by Prof. Jie Fu. I received my Bachelors degree in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, India. During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to worked on robot motion planning and cyber-physical systems that laid the foundation for my present research. I also started a student organization - Cognitive Robotics and Intelligent SysTems Lab (CRISTL) along with my colleague Monica Patel. At CRISTL, we developed and fabricated an autonomous robot Curio to be used for teaching at VIT.
