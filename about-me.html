Abhishek N. Kulkarni - About me

I am a third year Ph.D. student in Robotics Engineering Program at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), advised by Prof. Jie Fu. I am the graduate student representative from Robotics Engineering Program to the Graduate Student Government (GSG) since Spring 2019.

I received my Bachelors degree in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, India. During my undergraduate studies, I founded a student organization - Cognitive Robotics and Intelligent SysTems Lab (CRISTL) along with my colleague Monica Patel. At CRISTL, we developed an autonomous mobile robot Curio.
